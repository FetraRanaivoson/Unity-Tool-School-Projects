*** You can drag and drop game objects player onto the main movement controller to create a waypoint system
*** Any game objects you place on the player script will follow the system
*** The first waypoint you plug in is the first location the player will go through.
*** You can arrange the order of the waypoint system
*** You can delete a way point
*** The path is materialized by lines between each waypoint
*** The path is updated automatically as you arrange the order of the waypoint system

Plus
*** The player is a human character
*** The player follows the way point path just like a regular object
*** The player has animation: idle, walk, run
*** The player uses a navmesh system to move
*** Created a nice environment and add fog
