*** Create a platform with many players (sphere game objects)
*** Their colors will be randomized on the start of the game
*** They will have random speed and random direction
*** Their rigid body will be applied a force
*** When you click save, all players data will be saved onto a json file
*** When you click load, all players data will be retrieved so the players
will be back to their original state at the time of saving
*** The data retrieved are their name, poitision, rigid body's velocity and colors
